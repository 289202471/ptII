CapeCode ships with a number of models, some of which require hardware or software that is not shipped with CapeCode.

To run the models in CapeCode, click on the green triangle in the toolbar. The diagrams are fully editable, so feel free to explore.

Camera

Perhaps the most portable models are the models that use the camera.

If you do not have a camera, then a default stream of images will be generated.

  • Random: Cycle through image filters in a random order.
